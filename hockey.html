Hockey

Hockey is a fast-paced ball-and-stick sport that required co-ordination, stamina and agility. As a team sport, it's a great way to make friends and improve your communication and confidence.

As an all-rounder cardiovascular exercise, it also comes with increased strength and muscle tone, and is a proven stress-reliever.

Felice Albers (right) was recently named best player in the world by FIH, the international hockey federation.
