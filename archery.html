Archery

Archery draws on strength, precision, and coordination. It's a sport that's often been associated with women in fiction, from the Greek goddess Artemis to Katniss Everdeen.

Archery has also been proven to produce a meditative state of mind, improving your mood, easing anxiety and depression.

On the right is Phoebe Paterson Pine MBE, paralympic athlete and gold medallist at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, having first tried archery on a family holiday.
