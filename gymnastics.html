Gymnastics

Gymnastics brings together strength, stamina, flexibility, and artistry. It's a great sport for building strength and mobility. And who hasn't wanted to do a backflip?

Pictured to the right is Simone Biles, the most decorated gymnast in American sporting history.

But nurturing gymnastic abilities isn't just for elite athletes and children - adults can also benefit hugely from the increased co-ordination, agility, and power that gymnastics builds.

"Always work hard and have fun in what you do because I think that's when you're more successful. You have to choose to do it."
