Cycling

Cycling as a sport comes in a variety of different forms: road bicycle racing, track cycling, mountain bike racing, and BMX are just a few.

Cycling is also an environmentally friendly and economical form of transport, so it's a very practical sport to get started with!

Stationary cycling is advocated as a rehabilitative exercise, particularly for lower limb injury, due to its low impact on joints.

Kadeena Cox OBE (right) is a British parasport sprinter and cyclist, winning multiple gold medals at the 2016 and 2020 Paralympic Games.

"I love pushing boundaries and proving that things aren’t impossible. I love achieving things that nobody else has and proving to others that your circumstances and disabilities don’t mean that you can’t be extraordinary. "
