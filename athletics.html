Athletics

Athletics brings together a host of different sporting events, including track and field (which in itself contains a whole lot of running, jumping, and throwing events), roadrunning, and cross-country running.

Any rigorous exercise creates better brain activity by generating new nerve cells and blood vessels. It's also fantastic for boosting confidence, focus, and better sleep patterns.
