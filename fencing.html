Fencing

Fencing is a dynamic sport that derives from agility, strategy, and discipline. It's well-rounded and excellent for improving general fitness, focus, problem solving and stress reduction.

In competitive fencing, there are three main events: foil, épée, and sabre, each with slightly different rules and shapes of blades.

Sun Yiwen, pictured on the right, is a two-time team world champion épée fencer and in 2020 became the first Chinese fencer in history to win an Olympic gold medal in individual women's épée.

"I would not set a target and stop once I reach it. My goal has no limit. Although I don’t set my ambitions very high, I will keep on improving."
